---
title: Iconic
description: Customize your icons and their colors directly from the UI.
---

[Iconic](https://github.com/gfxholo/iconic) lets you customize icons and their colors directly from the UI, including tabs, files & folders, bookmarks, tags, properties, and ribbon commands.

Right-click any item in the sidebar, tab bar, or other supported areas to change its icon or color. This is useful for visually distinguishing important files or folders at a glance.
