---

[Bases CMS](https://github.com/davidvkimball/obsidian-bases-cms) lets you treat a grid of content like a content management system.

### Features

- **Bulk editing** — select multiple items and edit properties at once.
- **Inline renaming** — rename content right from the grid view.
- **Draft toggling** — toggle the draft status of any item.
- **Custom views** — configure the CMS view and add new views to your liking.

Bases CMS works together with [Home Base](/plugins/home-base/) to provide a directory of all your content.
