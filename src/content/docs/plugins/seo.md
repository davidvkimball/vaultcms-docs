The [SEO](https://github.com/davidvkimball/obsidian-seo) plugin provides a snappy audit of your content for search engine rankings and AI parsing.

### Features

- **Vault-wide audit** to get a quick snapshot of SEO health across all your content.
- **Per-post audit** to drill down into specific pages or posts.
- **Configurable checks** — turn off checks you don't care about or tweak the scoring logic.
